Benefits of receiving a massage:

 

Massage gives you a roadmap of stress areas in your body.а Many times people donТt know how much stress they are carrying until they receive a massage; then they are able to feel their tensions and let go of their stress.а As the massage therapist stretches and loosens muscles and connective tissues, stress and muscular tension are released.

Once you have an awareness of where stress lodges in your body, you can begin to do something about it.а You start to educate yourself and your body, and you reverse a negative cycle by incorporating stretching, massage, water, and watching your posture on an everyday basis.

Neuromuscular Massage, which is often used for injuries and conditions of pain, works at softening the muscles so that the therapist can come in more deeply to break up adhesions. This allows muscles to become really elastic, the way they should be. The body then may be freed of spasm and pain, increase its range of motion, and have the ability to avoid injury.

 

Physical:

  • Provides deep relaxation and stress reduction
  • Reduces muscle spasms, tension, and stiffness
  • Enables relief from entrapment of nerves in muscle
  • Provides greater joint mobility, flexibility, and range of motion
  • Promotes deeper and easier breathing
  • Improves circulation of blood, lymph fluids, and endorphins
  • Reduces blood pressure
  • Helps relieve tension headaches and effects of eye- strain
  • Enables healthier, better nourished skin
  • Improves posture
  • Enhances athletic performance
  • Helps with rehabilitation (post operative)
  • Helps treats injuries caused during sports or workouts
  • Reduces formation of scar tissue
  • Helps prevent injury
  • Alleviates discomfort during pregnancy

 

Mental and Emotional:

  • Strengthens the immune system
  • A relaxed state of alertness
  • Fosters peace of mind and feeling of well being
  • Feel Balanced
  • Enhances capacity for calm, clearer thinking and creativity
  • Encourages a greater ability to monitor and relieve stress
  • Reduces anxiety
  • Enhances self image
  • Encourages a sense of being unified and in harmony
  • Increases awareness of mind- body connection
  • Satisfies need for caring nurturing touch
  • Creates body awareness


People find that therapeutic massage can help with a wide range of medical conditions, including:

  • Allergies
  • Anxiety
  • Arthritis (both osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis)
  • Asthma and bronchitis
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Chronic and acute pain
  • Circulatory problems
  • Depression
  • Digestive disorders, including spastic colon, constipation and diarrhea
  • Gastrointestinal disorders, including spastic colon, colic and constipation
  • Headache
  • Immune function disorders
  • Insomnia
  • Myofascial pain (a condition of the tissue connecting the muscles)
  • Premature infants
  • Reduced range of motion
  • Sinusitis
  • Sports injuries, including pulled or strained muscles and sprained ligaments
  • Stress
  • Temporomandibular joint (TMJ) dysfunction
